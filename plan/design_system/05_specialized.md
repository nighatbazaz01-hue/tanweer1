# Tanweer Design System: Specialized Components

## 1. Dashboard Widgets

### KPI Cards
*   **Top row:** Title (Small gray), Value (Large blue), Percentage trend (Green/Red icon).
*   **Interactive:** Click card to navigate to detailed report.

### Charts (Recharts / Tremor)
*   **Line/Bar Charts:** For attendance and admission trends.
*   **Donut Charts:** For fee collection distribution.
*   **Colors:** Use primary Blue for data, Slate for background, and semantic Green/Red for status.

---

## 2. AI Assistant UI

### AI Chat Drawer
*   **Header:** "Tanweer Assistant" + sparkle icon.
*   **Message Bubbles:**
    *   **User:** Right-aligned, blue background, white text.
    *   **AI:** Left-aligned, light slate background, navy text.
*   **Input:** Multi-line text area with "Submit" sparkle icon.
*   **Feedback:** Small thumbs up/down icons at the bottom of every AI response.

### AI Suggestion Chips
*   Pill-shaped buttons above the input to trigger common queries ("Attendance today", "My schedule").

### AI Insights Badge
*   Subtle glowing blue badge on top of cards that contain AI-generated predictions (e.g. "Risk alert").

---

## 3. Navigation Components

### Sidebar
*   **Icons:** 20px Lucide icons.
*   **Labels:** 14px Medium.
*   **Active State:** Blue background (10% opacity) + Blue left-border (4px).

### Breadcrumbs
*   Gray-400 text, 12px, separated by ">" icon.
*   Current page is semi-bold navy.

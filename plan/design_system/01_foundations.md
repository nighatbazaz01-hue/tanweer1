# Tanweer Design System: Foundations

## 1. Color Palette

### Primary (Tanweer Blue)
*   **Primary:** #2563EB (Tailwind blue-600)
*   **Light:** #DBEAFE (Tailwind blue-100)
*   **Dark:** #1E3A8A (Tailwind blue-900)

### Secondary & Accents
*   **Neutral:** Slate Gray (#64748B)
*   **Success:** Emerald (#10B981)
*   **Warning:** Amber (#F59E0B)
*   **Danger:** Rose (#E11D48)

### System Backgrounds
*   **Light Mode:** White (#FFFFFF) / Surface (#F8FAFC)
*   **Dark Mode:** Deep Navy (#0F172A) / Surface (#1E293B)

---

## 2. Typography (Inter System)
*   **Font Family:** `Inter`, ui-sans-serif, system-ui.
*   **Headings:**
    *   H1: 32px / 2rem (Bold)
    *   H2: 24px / 1.5rem (Semibold)
    *   H3: 18px / 1.125rem (Semibold)
*   **Body:**
    *   Regular: 14px / 0.875rem (Normal)
    *   Small: 12px / 0.75rem (Normal)
*   **Code:** `JetBrains Mono` or system-mono.

## 4. Iconography
*   **Library:** Lucide React.
*   **Style:** Outline, 2px stroke width.

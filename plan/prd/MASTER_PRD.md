## 3. Product Vision & Value Proposition
Tanweer is not just a school management system; it is an AI-powered education ecosystem. 
*   **For Leadership:** Better decisions through data.
*   **For Teachers:** Reduced administrative workload.
*   **For Parents:** Real-time engagement with their child's education.
*   **For Students:** Improved performance through early risk detection.

## 4. Key Stakeholder Requirements

| Stakeholder | Key Requirement |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Investors** | Scalable multi-tenant SaaS model with 99.9% availability. |
| **School Owners** | Financial oversight and admission funnel optimization. |
| **Teachers** | Rapid attendance and AI lesson planning. |
| **Parents** | Secure mobile-friendly fee payments and child safety alerts. |

## 5. Security & Compliance
- **RLS Multi-tenancy:** Industry-standard tenant isolation.
- **Audit Trails:** Full accountability for critical data changes.
- **PII Protection:** Strict adherence to data privacy standards.

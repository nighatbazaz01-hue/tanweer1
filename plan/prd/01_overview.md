# Tanweer PRD: Overview, Personas, and Success Metrics

## 1. Product Vision
Tanweer is an AI-powered, multi-tenant SaaS School Management Platform designed to digitize and optimize every aspect of modern education. It aims to reduce administrative burdens for teachers, provide actionable insights for leadership, and keep parents and students deeply engaged in the learning journey.

## 2. Strategic Goals
*   **Scalability:** Support schools with 500 to 50,000+ students on a single unified platform.
*   **AI-First:** Leverage machine learning to predict risks (academic/financial) and automate content generation.
*   **Security:** Ensure enterprise-grade data isolation and protection using PostgreSQL RLS and modern auth.
*   **Efficiency:** Reduce teacher administrative time by 40% within the first year of adoption.

## 3. User Personas
*   **Super Admin:** Platform owner managing multiple schools, billing, and system health.
*   **School Admin / Principal:** Focused on school-wide performance, admissions, and financial stability.
*   **Teacher:** Focused on classroom management, attendance, grading, and lesson delivery.
*   **Student:** Focused on assignments, schedules, and academic progress.
*   **Parent:** Focused on their child's attendance, fees, and communication from the school.
*   **Accountant:** Focused on fee collection, discounts, and financial reporting.

## 4. Success Metrics (KPIs)
*   **User Adoption:** 90% of staff active on the platform daily.
*   **Admission Conversion:** 20% increase in lead-to-enrollment conversion via automated follow-ups.
*   **Financial Health:** 15% reduction in fee defaults through AI-powered early warnings.
*   **Platform Stability:** 99.9% uptime and <200ms API response time for core operations.
*   **AI Utility:** 50% of teachers using AI Assistant for lesson planning.

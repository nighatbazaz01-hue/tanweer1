1. TEACHER LEAVE MANAGEMENT
====================================

Current issue:

Teachers have no way to request leave.

Required:

Create Teacher Leave Request workflow.

Teacher can:

- Create leave request
- Select leave type
  - Sick Leave
  - Casual Leave
  - Emergency Leave
  - Half Day
  - Other

- Select:
  - From Date
  - To Date
  - Reason

Status:

- Pending
- Approved
- Rejected

↓

↓

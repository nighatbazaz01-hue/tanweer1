PHASE 6 — FOUNDATION SCHOOL HUMHAMA | MIND • BODY • SOUL AI ASSISTANT

CONTEXT

Foundation School Humhama follows a holistic education philosophy focused on:

1. MIND — Academic Excellence, Critical Thinking, Cambridge Learning Skills, Curiosity
2. BODY — Physical Health, Fitness, Sports, Nutrition, Lifestyle Habits
3. SOUL — Character Development, Emotional Wellbeing, Values, Leadership, Service, Reflection

The goal is NOT to create another generic AI chatbot.

The goal is to create a unique AI Assistant experience that reflects the school's educational philosophy and becomes a flagship feature during board demonstrations.

================================================================================
FEATURE NAME
================================================================================

Mind • Body • Soul AI Assistant

Displayed prominently within the AI Assistant section.

Add a visual selector with three cards/tabs:

🧠 Mind
💪 Body
❤️ Soul

Academic growth and intellectual development.

Suggested questions displayed as clickable chips:

Admin:
- Which students need academic intervention?
- Which grade is performing below expectations?
- What are the biggest academic risks this month?
- Summarize school-wide academic performance.
- Which subjects require support?

- Which classes are underperforming?
- Show academic concerns in my division.
- Which students need additional support?
- What trends should I focus on this month?

Teacher:
- Which students are falling behind?
- How can I improve engagement in my class?
- Suggest differentiated instruction strategies.
- Which students need extra support?

Parent:
- How can I support my child academically?
- What study habits should we improve?
- Which subject needs the most attention?

Student:
- Create a study plan for me.
- Which subject needs improvement?
- How should I prepare for exams?
- What are my academic strengths?

================================================================================
AI RESPONSE FORMAT
================================================================================

Each response should contain:

1. Insight
2. Why it matters
3. Recommended actions

Example:

Insight:
5 students in Grade 10-A show attendance below 85%.

Why it matters:
Consistent attendance strongly influences academic performance and wellbeing.

Recommended Actions:
• Schedule parent discussion
• Monitor attendance weekly
• Provide mentoring support
• Review transport or health concerns

================================================================================
AI RECOMMENDED ACTIONS PANEL
================================================================================

After every AI response, display actionable recommendations.

Examples:

Academic:
- Create Intervention Plan
- Schedule Parent Meeting
- Assign Mentor
- Monitor Progress

Wellbeing:
- Wellbeing Check-In
- Counsellor Referral
- Teacher Follow-Up

Leadership:
- Student Leadership Opportunity
- Community Service Activity
- Peer Mentoring Program

These actions may initially show informational dialogs and can later connect to workflows.

FINAL PRE-DEMO BUSINESS PROCESS AUDIT

IMPORTANT

DO NOT ADD FEATURES.

DO NOT REDESIGN UI.

DO NOT REFACTOR.

This is NOT a technical QA audit.

This is a SCHOOL OPERATIONS AUDIT.

The goal is to identify places where a school administrator, principal, VP, teacher, parent, or board member would ask:

"How is this actually done in the system?"
